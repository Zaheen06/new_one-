Build Your Future with Our Comprehensive Construction Internship Program

Step into the real world of construction with our industry-focused internship designed to turn aspiring architects and engineers into confident, job-ready professionals. This structured program covers every stage of the construction journey — from concept to completion — through three progressive learning phases: Pre-Construction, Construction, and Post-Construction.

Pre-Construction Phase

Laying the Foundation of Success

Gain a complete understanding of how projects begin and are planned to perfection. You’ll learn to:

  • •Master construction organization and site setup
  • •Handle project planning, scheduling, and programming
  • •Prepare estimations, costing, and material procurement
  • •Understand drawings, surveys, and reporting systems
  • •Learn material testing, quality control, and documentation
  • •Explore advanced techniques, environmental management, and sustainable design
  • •Get introduced to RERA, ISO standards, and real estate regulations

Construction Phase

Hands-On Site Experience

Bring theory to life with real-world construction practices and live site exposure. This phase covers:

  • •Excavation, foundation, RCC, and masonry works
  • •Plastering, waterproofing, plumbing, and electrical installations
  • •Flooring, painting, window and door fixing, and elevator systems
  • •Firefighting systems, safety protocols, and billing management
  • •Machinery handling and modern construction equipment
  • •Emphasis on Green Building Concepts and Energy-Efficient Techniques

Post-Construction Phase

Finishing and Handover Excellence

Learn what makes a project truly complete and client-ready. You’ll gain experience in:

  • •Repairs and maintenance management
  • •Final finishing works and amenities coordination
  • •Possession checklists and client handover processes
  • •Preparation of possession brochures and documentation
